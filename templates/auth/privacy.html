Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 — Last updated: February 2026

Mickey Legal ("Mickey") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Belgian data protection law.

1. Controller

Mickey Legal is the data controller for account and usage data. Contact: hello@askmickey.io

2. Data we collect

Account data: firm name, email address, name, country, VAT number. Used to manage your account.

Usage data: feature usage counts and timestamps (no content). Used to improve the service.

Documents you upload: processed to generate AI responses and immediately discarded. Not stored on our servers beyond the current session.

3. Cookies

Mickey uses a single session cookie strictly necessary for authentication. This cookie contains no personal data, is not shared with third parties, and is deleted when you sign out. No tracking, analytics or advertising cookies are used.

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) for account data, and legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for usage analytics. The session cookie is strictly necessary and does not require consent under Belgian cookie law.

5. Data retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months. Usage logs are retained for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or port your data, and to object to processing. Submit requests to hello@askmickey.io. You may also lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit.be).

7. Sub-processors

AI processing is performed via Anthropic's API (USA — Standard Contractual Clauses apply). Email delivery via Brevo (EU). No other sub-processors have access to your data.
